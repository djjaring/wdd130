3 DAYS TRIP-PAYAS RIVER

The PAYAS RIVER is an adrenaline-pumping adventure for thrill-seekers. This powerful river winds through rugged canyons and dense forests, offering breathtaking scenery and heart-pounding rapids. With a mix of challenging Class IV and exhilarating Class V rapids, this journey promises an unforgettable experience. Brace yourself for cascading waves, technical maneuvers, and exhilarating drops along the 5-mile stretch. Our expert guides will lead you through the rapids, ensuring both safety and excitement as you conquer the Payas River.

full DAY TRIP-NILOMBOT River

Embark on an unforgettable expedition down the Majestic NILOMBOT RIVER, a coursing waterway renowned for its dynamic landscapes and thrilling rapids. Carving through lush valleys and rugged terrain, this river offers an adrenaline-fueled journey for adventurers. With a mix of Class III to Class V rapids, including the infamous 'Twisting Serpent' and 'Thunderous Plunge,' rafters navigate intense currents and exhilarating drops. As you cascade through turbulent waters, be mesmerized by the stunning canyon vistas and the challenging yet rewarding experience this roaring river has to offer

1/2 DAY TRIP-BALOLING RIVER

Prepare for an adrenaline-charged voyage through the Rushing BALOLING RIVER, an awe-inspiring river path laden with adrenaline-pumping rapids and scenic wonders. Journeying through dramatic canyons and ancient rock formations, this river's Class IV and V rapids, such as the 'Roaring Behemoth' and 'Chaos Canyon,' promise an electrifying adventure. Brace against powerful waves, navigate challenging twists and turns, and feel the rush of conquering the unforgiving waters. Amidst the breathtaking scenery, the Rushing Fury Gorge offers an invigorating rafting experience that tests both skill and nerve
